About

Welcome to our seaside cafe.  Looking for great food with a quirky difference?


Chocolate Fish Cafe is located in a beautiful secluded area on the Wellington coastline offering an array of Kiwiana style food and a friendly enviroment for the whole family.

Our Food

We have taken Kiwiana to a new level - Seafood cooked on the bbq. Grab a blanket & enjoy the view!


All seafood is locally sourced where possible and we use free range produce + organic milk.
